Fishbrain

Catch the biggest fishbrain

Android — Frontend

During my time at Fishbrain I had an experience of working with multinational team. We were focused on delivering the best product for the anglers worldwide. The app consists of various features which help to find the spot to fish, check forecasts, share the experience and more.

As an Android engineer I was involved in developing of different parts of the product. Firstly I joined the team responsible for on boarding and subscriptions. We worked with Revenue Cat SDK, Firebase remote config and AWS Cognito API. After I switched to Social team which was responsible for delivering the best social experience. It included feed, messaging, third parties sharing, stories. We worked with Send Bird framework, Open GL and Open GL Vision for video processing and filters. And lastly I joined Utility team where most of the prediction and forecasting features are built and mantained. Here we work with Mapbox, Firebase Machine learning, AWS.
